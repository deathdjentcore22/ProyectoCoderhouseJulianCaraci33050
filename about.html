Our COFFEE

We take pride in our roots from Latin America. Our fathers came from South America with a dream: to share a cup of the best coffee and be a sort of counsel to people who needed it. While we are not psychologists we encourage you to find a stranger (or us!) to talk with. As for the coffee, we invested our time and efforts into researching which beans are best suited for each season, for each moment, for each brew. We have suppliers pick their best beans from the harvest and ship them to us straight from their hometown, be it Colombia, Brazil, Ethiopia or Uganda.

We the owners want to make this place feel as much a home as your own is, so relax, order some coffee, put on some music, work on your projects in our tables/couches (we have plugs for your computer!), bring your dog so he can enjoy a healthy cup of milk while you drink your cup.
